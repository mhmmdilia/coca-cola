A Purpose-Driven History

The Coca‑Cola Company’s purpose is to refresh
the world and make a difference and we have
remained true to that purpose for 137 years.

Our strategy is centered around people—our
consumers and employees—and driving
sustainable solutions that build resilience into
our business to respond to current and future
challenges, while creating positive change for
the planet.
